How to BUY a book

Searching
STEP 1

The first step to buying a book, is using the search bar. First, pick a category to search from using the drop-down. The options are: Book Title, ISBN, Course Code, Course Title. Based on the search category you chose, type in text representing the category, for the book you are looking for. After that, hit the Search button and the results should load!

You don't need to type in the full information, our matching system can detect part of the information and get you the right book!
Picking a Book
STEP 2

Once you search, the book results should load. You should be able to identify your book based on the image of the book in the entries. There is also supplementary information about the author, course title, and price. Once you've found a book you like, click on it to take you to our buyer page.

If you don't find the book in our Search Results, you may need to check your data entry! Otherwise, there are no sellers of your book.
Bidding on a Book
STEP 3-A

On the buyer's page, more information should be shown to you. You can confirm the quality of the book by clicking through the image carousel displayed. If you are confident that you want the book, please place a bid.

Do not place a bid lower than the Lowest Ask, or higher than the Buy Now price.
Buying a Book
STEP 3-B

Instead of competing in the bidding system for a book, we also provide the option to buy. The seller will have a Buy Now price that you can meet. Hit the button if you'd like to get the book straight away. If you do, then all you need to do is wait for the seller's confirmation.

If you navigate to the My Profile section, you should see a record of your bought book under 'Purchased Books'.
Bidding Confirmation
STEP 4

Once you have successfully placed a bid, you can navigate to the My Profile section and see your bid under 'Current Bids' Once the seller has seen your bid, they can accept or reject the bid. If they accept it, then you have the option to confirm your bid. .

Use the Confirm/Deny option to confirm whether you truly want the book at your bidded price.
Payment
STEP 5

After confirming the book, you will be sent through to a page to enter your Venmo information. After that, we will send a Venmo request to your account so you can pay the buyer!

You have successfully bought a book! You can co-ordinate the physical book handover! Thank you for using Book Exchange.
