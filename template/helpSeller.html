How to SELL a book

Searching
STEP 1

The first step to buying a book, is using the search bar. First, pick a category to search from using the drop-down. The options are: Book Title, ISBN, Course Code, Course Title. Based on the search category you chose, type in text representing the category, for the book you are looking for. After that, hit the Search button and the results should load!

You don't need to type in the full information, our matching system can detect part of the information and get you the right book!

Accepting or Denying an Offer
STEP 3

On the My Profile section, if a buyer has placed a bid, you should be able to see it under the 'Current Listings' tab. You can choose to accept or deny their offer.

If you deny the offer, the next highest bid will appear. The bids will refresh once a higher bid is placed.
Payment
STEP 4

If you accept the bid, the buyer has the option to Confirm their purchase. If they do confirm, then the book is sold, and you can arrange payment and physical exchange!

You have successfully sold a book! Thank you for using Book Exchange.
